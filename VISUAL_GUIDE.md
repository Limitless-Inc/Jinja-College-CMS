## 🎨 COLOR SCHEME

### Primary Colors
- **Deep Blue** (#1e40af) - Primary buttons, active states
- **Dark Navy** (#1f2937) - Sidebar background
- **Light Gray** (#F5F7FA) - Page background
- **White** (#ffffff) - Cards, modals

### Status Colors
- **Green** (#10b981) - Success, excellent, 90%+ attendance
- **Blue** (#3b82f6) - Good, information
- **Orange** (#f59e0b) - Warning, fair, 70-89% attendance
- **Red** (#ef4444) - Error, poor, below 70% attendance
- **Purple** (#8b5cf6) - Special features

### Text Colors
- **Dark** (#1f2937) - Headings, primary text
- **Gray** (#6b7280) - Secondary text, labels
- **Border** (#e5e7eb) - Dividers, borders
